About Me

I am a competitive junior Software Engineer, climbing my way through the software developer industry.

All my life, I have had a passion for any STEM related field. I took advanced Physics and Calculus classes in High School, and I majored in Physics in college. However, I am a very competitive person by nature so I had decided to take some time away from the STEM field to play competitive soccer I had the privilege to travel to different locations and play. I even went to Ireland to play for some time, where our entire team all lived in one apartment building. After being restricted on how much I can play during the pandemic, I was able to find my way back to STEM fields.

I thrive in environments where I am constantly challenging myself, and pushing myself to learn new things. This led to me getting into software development. I enrolled into App Academy, and spent 60+ hours a week for 6 months learning and developing the skills necessary to become a successful software engineer. I learned programming languages such as JavaScript and Python; and I learned how to use a plethora of frameworks and libraries such as ExpressJS, Flask, PUG, Jinja, PostgreSQL, React, and Redux. I have built 4 full-stack projects using all of these frameworks and libraries. 2 of them on my own, and 2 of them together with other programmers as a team.

I have come a long way to get to where I am, but I know my journey is not over. My competitive nature does not allow me to relax until I am the very best programmer that I can be, and my thirst for knowledge keeps pushing me to overcoming new challenges. I plan on continuing to learn new technologies and doing what I can to build software that is both enjoyable and practical.
